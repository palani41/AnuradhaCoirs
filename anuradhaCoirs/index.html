Coconut Fibre

Coconut fiber is commonly used as a growing medium in horticulture. It is often mixed with soil or other growing substrates to improve aeration, water retention, and nutrient uptake for plants. Coir pots, coir mats, and coir logs are popular horticultural products made from coconut fiber

Coconut peat Coin

To use coconut coir coins, you typically place them in a tray or container and add warm water. Allow the discs to absorb the water and expand fully before planting seeds or transplanting seedlings. Once hydrated, the coir discs can be easily handled and planted

Grow bag

To use a grow bag, simply fill it with a suitable growing medium, such as potting mix or a soilless mix like coco coir or peat moss. Plant your seeds or seedlings according to the spacing and depth requirements of the specific plant species.
